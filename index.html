About Our Department

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science at Global University. Our department is dedicated to providing world-class education in computer science and information technology. We strive to develop skilled professionals who can contribute to the rapidly evolving field of technology and innovation.

With a team of experienced faculty members and modern infrastructure, we offer comprehensive programs that blend theoretical knowledge with practical skills. Our graduates have gone on to work in leading tech companies and startups across the globe.

Courses Offered

Introduction to Programming

Learn the fundamentals of programming using Python. This course covers variables, loops, functions, and basic data structures.

Web Development

Master HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build interactive websites. Create responsive and user-friendly web applications.

Database Management

Understand relational databases and SQL. Learn to design, create, and manage databases efficiently.

Data Structures and Algorithms

Explore essential data structures like arrays, linked lists, and trees. Learn algorithm design and optimization techniques.
